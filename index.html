FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book an appointment in advance? +
We recommend booking at least a day in advance, especially for bridal and special occasion services. For regular services, walk-ins are welcome based on availability. You can book via WhatsApp, phone, or our online form.
What is included in a bridal makeup package? +
Our bridal packages include pre-bridal consultation, HD or airbrush makeup, saree/lehenga draping, bridal hairstyling, and a trial session. Premium packages also include engagement and reception makeup. Please call us for detailed package information and pricing.
Do you use skin-safe and certified beauty products? +
Absolutely! We only use internationally certified and dermatologist-tested products from brands like MAC, L'Oréal Professional, and Schwarzkopf. Your skin's safety is our top priority.
What are your opening hours? +
We are open 7 days a week from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM. On special occasions and festivals, we may extend hours — please confirm via WhatsApp for last-minute slots.
Do you offer home service for makeup? +
Yes, we do offer home service for bridal and special event makeup within Ludhiana, subject to artist availability. Additional charges apply. Contact us via WhatsApp to discuss your requirements and get a quote.

Our Story

Born from a Passion for Beauty

Nour Salon was founded with a singular vision — to bring world-class luxury beauty services to the heart of Ludhiana. "Nour" means light in Arabic, and we believe in helping every woman discover her own inner radiance and light.

Starting as a small, intimate beauty parlour in Model Town, we've grown into one of Ludhiana's most trusted and premium beauty destinations. Our journey has been one of continuous learning, creativity, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Today, we are proud to have transformed thousands of women — from everyday clients seeking a fresh look to brides on the most special day of their lives.

🌸

Our Mission

To provide every woman with a luxurious, personalised beauty experience that enhances her natural beauty, boosts her confidence, and makes her feel truly special — every single visit.

👑

Our Vision

To be Punjab's most loved and trusted luxury beauty salon — a place where art, skill, and heartfelt care come together to create transformative beauty experiences that last a lifetime.
